Our Strategies

Land Rights

VMKS advocates for formal land titles for the tribal communities who have historically lackedownership records, leading to land usurpation by outsiders. It fights against the declaration of tribalcommunities as forest encroachers and aims to secure sufficient land for agriculture and fodder production,with a particular focus on women's land rights.

Agriculture and Livelihood Issues

VMKS works towards addressing the challenges of limited access tominor forest produce, low agricultural yields due to topography and rainfall constraints, and restricted grazingopportunities for animals. It aims to improve livelihood options beyond low-productivity agriculture and rurallabor by exploring non-farm rural employment opportunities.

Migration Problem

The organization recognizes the negative impact of short-term migration on educationand advocates for sustainable livelihood options within the Vagad region. VMKS aims to address the issuesfaced by tribal families working in low-paying occupations outside their communities, promoting long-term security and improved prospects for their children's education.

Conservation and Promotion of Natural Resources

VMKS strives to secure individual and community forest rights allocations to protect forests and ensure that local communities benefit from their resources sustainably.It opposes bureaucratic hurdles in obtaining forest entitlements and works towards restoring the sense of ownership and belonging among young tribal's.

Timely Access to Government Schemes

VMKS advocates for the full and timely implementation ofgovernment schemes, including ration, pension, housing, toilets, and the Mahatma Gandhi National RuralEmployment Guarantee Act (MGNREGA), within the predominantly tribal Dungarpur district

Leadership Skills and Cultural Change

Recognizing the lack of awareness and limited local leadership in tribal society, VMKS aims to develop leadership skills and promote cultural preservation. It emphasizes theimportance of community engagement, advocating for the tribal community's issues at various levels, including panchayat's, blocks, and district administrations.
